## Customizing Your Workshop

To customize the workshop schedule:

1. **Choose Your Focus**

   - Frontend-only workshop
   - Smart contracts-only workshop
   - Balanced full-stack workshop

2. **Set Daily Goals**

   - Define learning objectives for each day
   - Allocate time based on complexity
   - Include hands-on exercises

3. **Mix and Match**
   - Combine modules from Frontend and Smart Contracts tabs
   - Add breaks and Q&A sessions
   - Include project time for practice
